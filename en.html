Bed & Breakfast

Welcome to Andersen's House!

We live in the countryside in quiet and scenic surroundings - not far from the Messecentre, BOXEN, Fiskesøen and Momhøj Nature Center.

We appreciate hospitality and have furnished an entire floor in the garage building with 3 rooms and the possibility of extra beds. Our guests have their own entrance and can stay undisturbed. It is suitable when several people are traveling together because there is a common living room and we can arrange the rooms with a combination of beds as required. We ourselves like going on trips with friends and appreciate good facilities where you can enjoy yourself together.

You can either rent the entire home or individual rooms.
